Barca goalkeeper

Marc-André ter Stegen



Marc-André ter Stegen is a German professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for La Liga club Barcelona and the Germany national team. Known for his reflexes, passing, and ball-playing ability, he is considered one of the best goalkeepers in the world. After four seasons in the Bundesliga with Borussia Mönchengladbach, making 108 league appearances, he joined Barcelona for €12 million in 2014. He won the treble in his first season in Spain, playing for Barcelona in Copa del Rey and UEFA Champions League. Ter Stegen represented Germany at several youth levels and made his senior international debut in 2012. He was part of the German squads that reached the semi-finals of UEFA Euro 2016 and won the 2017 FIFA Confederations Cup, and was also a member of the German side that took part at the 2018 FIFA World Cup.
